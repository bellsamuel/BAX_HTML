People and Things:

Over the last few years, through encounters via the internet, or from shop vists and the local community we've developed a rich source of friends and contacts.


From guitar builders supplies, custom guitar builders or live music venues, here are some of our favorites.

Players:

  • Wilco

    An awesome alternative rock band based in Chicago, Illinois.

  • Drive By Truckers

    Luminary southern rock maestro's, hailing from Athens, GA.

  • My Morning Jacket

    Rock and Roll band of diverse talent from Louisville, Kentucky.

  • Justin Townes Earl

    Poetic singer-songwriter from Nashville, TN.

  • Brittany Howard

    Songwriter, guitar player and vocalist for Alabama Shakes out of Athens, AL.

  • R.E.M

    Iconic rock band from Athens, GA.

    Tuscon, Arizona's prodical songwriting talent.

  • Of Montreal

    Athens. Indie. Psychedelic mastery.
